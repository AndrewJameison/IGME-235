DayZ

An equal partner in the rise of the survival-crafting genre was DayZ: the wildly popular mod for the military simulation game Arma II. It introduced more realistic survival mechanics beyond hunger and thirst, such as limb fractures and infection. Server hosts could easily customize the mod to fit their own preferences, letting players pick different servers that prioritized base building, PvP, or survival to name a few.
